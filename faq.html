Frequently Asked Questions:


Q. How can I rent a cycle/scooter?

A. You can pay for your rental on the register page. When you pay, you will be given a code that you can put into the machine located at the specified location.


Q. What happens if I get a flat tyre?

A. If you get a flat tyre, please stop using the bike/scooter immediately and phone support at 0800 CYCLES. We will give you a full refund within 1-2 days.


Q. What if it starts to rain and my plans change?

A. If your plans change due to poor weather, please contact us at 0800 CYCLES and we will give you a full refund within 1-2 days.


Q. Am I covered by insurance if I have an accident?

A. We are not liable for any injuries sustained from using our bikes/scooters. You will be covered by ACC or your insurance provider for any injuries sustained using our services.
